Fundamentals of Computer Engineering

1. Description

FCE is a subject designed to cover computer engineering from a global point of view, as an academic discipline and as a profession. It covers theoretical foundations, including history, information and data, and information processing, management and transmission; and practical abilities, including team work, document elaboration and oral presentation on a topic, as well as the development of a website. Moreover, this subject advocates for the development of communication, team work and technical skills, while also promoting ethical compromise.
