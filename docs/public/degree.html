Business and Data Analytics + Computer Engineering

Purpose

The purpose of this degree is to integrate two of the degrees with the greatest professional projection, incorporating learning through challenges and the integration of different sciences (Law, Business, Economics and Government) through the College to form interdisciplinary profiles that are experts in business strategy, big data, software and programming, who are capable of facing the new challenges of a world marked by the digital revolution (ufv.es).

Structure

The structure can be analyzed from two different points of view:
In terms of degrees, the business and data analytics degree has the status of mother degree, while computer engineering is the one that is built on top. For that reason, business and data analytics subjects are concentrated on the first years, while computer engineering is equitably distributed among the 5 years and a half that the degree lasts.
If we analyze the structure from the perspective of years, the whole double degree is distributed along 5 years and a half. All years have subjects corresponding to both degrees except for the last half-year, in which only subjects from the computer engineering degree are coursed. Furthermore, during the first semester of the first year, as part of the business and data analytics degree, the six fundamental subjects of the UFV College are coursed, as this degree belongs to it.
